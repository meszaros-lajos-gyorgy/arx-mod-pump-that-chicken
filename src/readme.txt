--------------------
 Pump that chicken!
--------------------

An Arx Fatalis mod that makes the "clicking on chickens" mechanism a bit more interesting

Don't click on the chicken too fast!

--------------
 Installation
--------------

Unpack the contents of this zip and add it into
your Arx Fatalis/Arx Libertatis game's root folder

-----------
 Uninstall
-----------

Remove the following files from your Arx Fatalis/Arx Libertatis game's root folder:
- graph/obj3d/interactive/npc/chicken_base/chicken_base.asl
- sfx/deflate.wav

Or if you don't have any other mods installed then just delete the "graph" and "sfx" folders
